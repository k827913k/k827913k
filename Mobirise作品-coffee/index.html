The evolution of eating methods

The Arabs know how to dry and roast green coffee beans, mash them with tools, and then boil them with water, so they get more pure coffee. At first, the Arabs used coffee as a refreshing stomach medicine, but gradually it was widely used as a refreshing drink.

The popularity of coffee

          Because Muslims are strictly forbidden to drink alcohol in the Holy Qur'an, the coffee can be used as an exciting drink instead of alcohol, which prompts Arabs to consume large amounts of coffee. The Muslim holy place Mecca has always been the center of faith for Arabs. Frequent believers have come and coffee has gradually spread to Egypt, Syria, Iran, Turkey and other places through believers. Therefore, religion is an important factor that promotes the widespread popularity of coffee in the Arab world.
